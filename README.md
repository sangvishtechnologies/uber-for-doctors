## What is Uber for Doctor?
“[Uber for Doctor](https://sangvish.com/uber-for-doctors/)” describes a model of delivering healthcare service where doctors attend to patients after being connected to them via an App like an Uber rider is connected with an Uber driver. 

These are health apps through which patients can order doctors’ appointments for a face-to-face or teleconsultation without visiting clinics or hospitals for long hours. Similar to how Uber ensures the availability of convenient means of transport, there is what is referred to as the [Uber for Doctor App](https://sangvish.com/uber-for-doctors/) where the user can find doctors, view doctors’ profiles, make an appointment with him or her, and get prescription without leaving the comfort of their homes.

Patients and doctors are notified of an appointment change, new appointment, or follow-up in real-time through notification.

* **Ratings & Reviews**

These services enable patients to also make their comments on the services offered by doctors. It provides the opportunity to determine trustworthy professionals and to allow other users to make the right choice in the selection of qualified specialists.

* **Emergency Medical Services**

Some applications contain icons that allow users to summon an ambulance instantly or call an emergency doctor.

* **Doctor Onboarding System**

The app is easy to use to onboard healthcare professionals; it promptly helps doctors register, upload the required documents, and begin practice.

* **In-app Chat**

Many of them consist of messaging functions by which patients can ask some additional or follow-up questions, which saves time considerably.
